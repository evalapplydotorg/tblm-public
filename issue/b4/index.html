Two Poems

Manjiri Indurkar
12 Dec 2017 / Issue B4 / poetry

**Diabetes at a Birthday Party ** On a channel, a day late in celebrating Lata Mangeshkar’s birthday, plays a song where Sadhana promises to haunt her lover and Ma pierces a needle in her large stomach pushing insulin into her body diabetes, she reminds me, will eat your bones. if you ever look at pictures of those suffering from rheumatoid arthritis you will find the bones parting ways from each other.
