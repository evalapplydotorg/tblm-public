Two poems – by Nabanita Kanungo

( top ^ )

SEASON Death is a sure scent beyond the compound and his season has arrived about a month ago. There are no cats in the vicinity. Only a dangerous number of young dogs. The regular mate isn’t around. Her owner has taken her away on a holiday. One day, his risks at luring someone else paid off and the ensuing rituals offered neighbours hope that the embarrassment may stop. But the scuffles and races must have been too brief
