Equal
Lepota L. Cosmo
/ ~1 min. read / / Jun 27, 2014 / / Issue 6 // poetry /

Equal

between amber
and blue eyes
seven warriors of
thistle
and heat of the sun
as three hummingbirds
in your eyelashes.
