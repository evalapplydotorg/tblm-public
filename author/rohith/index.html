Three Poems

Rohith
12 Dec 2018 / Issue C3 / poetry

Premonitions 1 A white room with monitors an otherworldly space in winter I see you from the flip side of the soundproof glass-wall; tubes inserted from every side screens playing an unscripted language in motion. Your body is immobile – but your eyes, they dart in all directions in their yearning to remember – our eyes meet for a moment, I read the question inscribed in your glance – what if death
