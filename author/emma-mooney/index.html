Dear America

Emma Mooney
03 Mar 2018 / Issue B7 / poetry

_(To listen to the poet read the poem, click here.) _ * We ken we’ve said aw this afore But, please, lend us yer ear fur a minute. Aye, we’re jist a wee country, But we’re a wee country wi a big hert, An when Thomas Hamilton Stormed intae a schuil gym An shot dead 16 bairns an their teacher, Oor hert wis broken. Nuthin we cuid dae wid bring the wee yins back,
