Yellow Laddoos
Balwant Gargi (tr. C. Christine Fair)
May 25, 2021 / Issue 52 / fiction (~9 min. read)
Puro was about to leave for the fields when she said to Jangir, “Will you come with me? I’ll give you laddoos to eat.” Jangir had been playing with a soapnut in the lane. He swept up the soapnut, put it in his pocket and set off with Puro. Puro was a young jat girl who danced the giddha and who, while playing on the swing, always soared higher than anyone else.
