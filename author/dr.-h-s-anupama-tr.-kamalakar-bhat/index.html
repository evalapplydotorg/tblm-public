This Poem is Deliberately Untitled

Dr. H S Anupama (tr. Kamalakar Bhat)
05 May 2021 / Issue E2 / poetry

If my clothes get torn, I mend them If soiled, I wash them If I find no clothes I will walk nude like you, Akka, but How do I wash the dirt on a dress not worn? If my sandal’s straps come undone, I’ll repair them Left with a single shoe, will put it away I could even cross the thorny, stony paths barefoot, but If the path begins to devour the feet, Akka
