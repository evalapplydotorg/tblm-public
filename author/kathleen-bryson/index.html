Three poems

Kathleen Bryson
10 Oct 2021 / Issue E5 / poetry

First Person I’m sure I initially saw you, the mandrill, in a children’s illustrated photographic book or possibly in a National Geographic pictorial and I always think the same thing about your cream teeth and Union Jack black red and blue mask I always think the same thing you are beautiful you are terrifying I think, all hail the surreal primate troll I think, the mammal clown with garish smeared soft
