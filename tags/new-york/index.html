The Mummy’s Bitter And Melancholy Exile

Norman Lock
01 Jan 2014 / Issue 4 / fiction

_The story was first published in Cranky Literary Journal, and subsequently appeared in Lock’s newest short story collection, Love Among the Particles, published in May, 2013, by Bellevue Literary Press, New York City. _ _Osiris, God of the Dead _ The Mummy is invited to speak on the radio. He is driven from his apartment house through the autumn streets, toward the broadcasting studio. He looks through the limousine window at the street-cleaners sweeping the fallen leaves.
